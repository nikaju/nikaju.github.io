Data Science Projects

Price optimisation

When? 2025

Why? For fun :)

Context? Many second-hand retail apps offer a “push up” feature that temporarily boosts an item's visibility, often leading to a spike in views and faster sales. I explored how to price this feature more effectively using a mock catalogue of 238 items and over 13 million listings. While the default price is €2 per “push up,” I questioned whether this flat rate was optimal.

What did I do?I developed a data-driven method to assess the current monetization strategy for the “push up” feature and proposed improvements to maximize platform revenue while considering long-term seller engagement.

Take-away message: Dynamic pricing outperforms flat pricing when it comes to both profitability and fairness

Why it matters? Smart pricing increases company revenue and builds trust with sellers by offering fairer, more tailored options
