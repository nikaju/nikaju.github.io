Data Science Projects

Price optimisation

When? 2025

Why? For fun :)

Context? One of the promotion features in second-hand retail apps is the “push up” feature that boosts item visibility. As a result, the item that is up for sale will get significantly more views (on average). Currently, it costs €2 to buy the “push up” feature, but that is not the optimal amount to be charged.

What did I do?I came up with a data-driven approach to evaluate the existing “push up” monetization strategy and gave suggestions on how to improve the monetization of the platform and its long-term effects.

Take-away message: Dynamic pricing of a feature is a better option than universal pricing

Why important? Optimises revenue of the company, as well as being fair to the sellers.
